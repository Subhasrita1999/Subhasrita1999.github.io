RFWE Programme at District Fisheries Office, Khurdha

Insights into local fisheries practices and challenges were gained through engagement with fish farmers, Self-Help Groups (SHGs), and entrepreneurs. Knowledge of government schemes supporting fish farmers was acquired, enhancing understanding of rural fisheries management, policy frameworks, and community dynamics.

ELP at CoF, OUAT, Berhampur

Participation in the Experiential Learning Programme on Fish Processing Technology (ELP-PHT) involved developing and marketing fish-based products such as Fish Curry, Fish Cutlets, Dry Fish, Dry Shrimp, and Prawn Pickles. Key learnings included quality standards, product preparation, and strategic marketing. Insights were gained into supply chain management and consumer preferences, enhancing technical skills and entrepreneurial acumen in fish processing technology.

In-plant attachment at SHIMPO SEAFOODS PVT. LTD., Bhubaneswar

Exposure to the intricacies of processing and exporting marine frozen products was gained during the internship at Shimpo Sea Foods Private Limited. Involvement included the entire fish processing continuum, from raw material procurement to exportation. Responsibilities encompassed quality assurance, including checks for product grades, colors, and sizes. Key processing activities performed included beheading, dressing, grading, and maintaining regulatory compliance. Insights were gained into buyer’s inspections, container loading, and microbiological and organoleptic tests. This experience provided a robust technical foundation and understanding of efficient fish processing operations.


M.F.Sc. Dissertation at ICAR-CIFA, Bhubaneswar

Conducted a dissertation on aquaculture at ICAR-CIFA, focusing on the impact of ammonia toxicity on *Catla catla* fry. The research involved evaluating physiological and biochemical responses, including growth metrics, enzyme activities, and protein levels. Comprehensive data analysis was performed to assess the effects of varying ammonia concentrations on fish health and development. This work provided a deep understanding of ammonia's impact on aquaculture and & contributed valuable insights into mitigating toxic effects in freshwater fish.
